The goal of Hector Cove is to create a small area for our presentation that shows off some fun gameplay. The premise involves a small flock of birds, called "Hectors". The hectors will react to the players actions. The players will learn that they can herd the hectors into two areas on the beach. In one area, Players will see the hectors fly off to a distant nest, avoiding the "Shale", a water creature who attempts to eat them. In the other area the hectors are not so lucky and the Shale has a meal. A gentle meal- not too intense of course.
