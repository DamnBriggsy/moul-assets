Kahlo is a wind swept age where regular tournaments between opposing teams of avatars are held. The tournaments take the form of a "capture the flame" competition where the object is to retrieve three flames of different colors to the home torch before the other team. When the home torch is complete it will explode into a spectacular pyrotechnic display, ending the game. It is a great honor to be the one who completes the flame and ignites the home torch. Crowds of avatars fill the spectator areas in anticipation of the ending when they are treated to the spectacular display.
